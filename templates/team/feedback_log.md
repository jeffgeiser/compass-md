# Team Feedback Log

Structured log of feedback observations. Agents append here when feedback is given on team outputs. Humans append directly after review sessions. Used to identify patterns that should update guidelines or criteria.

Format:

```
## [YYYY-MM-DD] | [Output type] | [Logged by]

**What was reviewed:** [brief description]
**Feedback source:** [leader / client / agent-observed correction]
**Observation:** [what was noted]
**Implication:** [what this might mean for guidelines or criteria]
**Status:** open | incorporated | dismissed
```
