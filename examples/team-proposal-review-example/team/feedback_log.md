## [2026-04-18] | Proposal review | Agent 2

**What was reviewed:** Proposal to [Prospect-009] — mid-market fintech, 180 engineers
**Feedback source:** Jordan review session, 2026-04-18
**Observation:** ROI section used Foundry's internal benchmark data ("teams reduce MTTR by 40%") rather than numbers derived from Prospect-009's discovery call. Jordan asked the rep to replace with the actual incident frequency and cost numbers from the discovery call.
**Implication:** Criteria gap — ROI clarity criteria says "use numbers derived from prospect's own context" but it's not explicit enough that benchmark numbers from Foundry fail this test. Candidates for criteria update: add "Foundry-provided benchmarks ≠ prospect-derived numbers" as an explicit note.
**Status:** open
