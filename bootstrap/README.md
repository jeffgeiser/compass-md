# Bootstrap Prompts

Prompts for initializing a Compass from existing material. Use these when starting from scratch or when you want to enrich an existing Compass quickly.

---

## When to bootstrap

A blank Compass is better than no Compass, but agents need *something* to read for the convention to be useful from session one. Bootstrap prompts help you get to a useful first draft in 30-60 minutes instead of starting completely empty.

Bootstrap outputs are always drafts. Review carefully before saving to your Compass. Models can produce plausible-sounding-but-wrong inferences, especially about voice and perspective. Your job is to keep what's accurate and cross out what isn't.

---

## Available prompts

### [`voice-from-writing-samples.md`](voice-from-writing-samples.md)

**Use when:** You have existing writing (emails, posts, briefs) and want to extract a `voice.md` from it rather than starting blank.

**Input:** 5-15 samples of your writing across contexts.

**Output:** A draft `self/voice.md` with observed patterns, confidence notes, and specific examples.

**Time:** 20-30 minutes (10 minutes gathering samples, 15-20 minutes reviewing and editing output).

---

### [`interview-perspectives.md`](interview-perspectives.md)

**Use when:** You want to populate `self/perspectives/` for topics you're opinionated about but haven't written down.

**Input:** Just yourself — the prompt conducts a conversational interview.

**Output:** Draft perspective files, additions to `preferences.md`, and possibly entries for `decisions.md`.

**Time:** 45-60 minutes for a meaningful interview.

---

### [`team-guidelines-from-history.md`](team-guidelines-from-history.md)

**Use when:** Starting a team Compass and you have existing material — past decisions, completed work, meeting notes — to extract guidelines from.

**Input:** Examples of the team's past outputs and any written guidelines that exist.

**Output:** A draft `team/guidelines.md` and possibly a `team/criteria/` file.

**Time:** 30-45 minutes.

---

### [`facts-from-resume.md`](facts-from-resume.md)

**Use when:** You want a quick-start `self/facts.md` without writing it from scratch.

**Input:** Your resume, LinkedIn profile, or any "about me" document.

**Output:** A structured draft `self/facts.md`.

**Time:** 10-15 minutes.

---

## After bootstrapping

1. Review each output file carefully
2. Cross out anything that doesn't ring true
3. Add anything that's missing or that the model wouldn't have known
4. Save the edited version to your Compass
5. Use it for a week, then check whether agents' output feels right — if not, the relevant Compass file probably needs refinement
